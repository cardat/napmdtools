The National Air Pollution Monitoring Database (NAPMD)

3 News

3.1 2025-11-18

  • Added 2023 and 2024 data for all states
  • Amendments to date and time fields
    • Fixed incorrect date_time_utc UTC timestamps for Victorian observations for 2021 and 2022 data
    • Fixed incorrect date and hour fields for NSW 2018 hourly observations (hour incorrectly specified one hour early) - previously labelled with local time between 2007-12-31 23:00 to 2008-12-31 21:00
    • Fixed incorrect year labels for the last hour of each year in the hourly observation tables for NSW, NT, SA
      • incorrect year is that of the following year (e.g. 1995-12-31 23:00 was incorrectly labelled as year = 1996)
  • Alignment with standard variable and unit naming
    • Amendment of variable ‘wind_dir_stddev’ to ‘wind_direction_std_dev’ and units ’w/m*’ to ‘W_per_m2’ for WA 2021-2022 observation data
    • Amendment of variable ‘solar_radiation’ to ‘solar’ for NT 2020-2022 observation data

3.2 2025-10-20

  • Added 2009 data for Queensland

3.3 2025-07-23

  • Amended data for ACT, replacing misidentified rolling means with 1-hrly averaged data
    • CO (replaced 8hr-rolling means with 1-hrly averages) for 2011 to 2020
    • PM10 (replaced 24hr-rolling means with 1-hrly averages) for 2011 to 2020
    • PM2.5 (replaced 24hr-rolling means with 1-hrly averages) for 2014 to 2020, removed 24hr-rolling means from 2013

3.4 2024-11-18

  • Replaced 2020 QLD data with full year of validated data
  • Removed irrelevant monitor sites (non-air pollution monitoring)
  • Fixed station_id and station for observations attached to the incorrect set of coordinates for that site (where station has moved)

3.5 2024-11-14

  • Added 2021 and 2022 data for all states
  • Updated stations to reflect station movement

3.6 2024-11-05

  • Corrected datetime fields for NT 2011-2017 where observations with hour 0 were assigned to the wrong date
  • Corrected datetime fields for ACT, NT and WA to be hour-beginning in line with other states and territories

3.7 Archived

3.7.1 2023-11-19

  • Removed all non-1hrly averages from ap_monitor_data_master table
  • Corrected date_time_utc field
  • Removed QLD observations in 2020 where value = -9999

3.7.2 2022-01-20

  • Licence for Tas EPA data updated.
