7.2 National Air Technical Advisory Group (NATAG)

The National Air Quality Technical Advisory Group (NATAG) is the national group who provides advice on air pollution policy and standards. Membership is drawn from state and territory agencies and the federal government.

7.3 Air Quality Legislation

The National Environment Protection (Ambient Air Quality) Measure (NEPM) sets out air pollution concentration standards and averaging periods.
